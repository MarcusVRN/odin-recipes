Marshmallow hot fudge sauce

Description

A quick, easy, and smooth fudge sauce that's super easy to make with marshmallows, milk, and chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 30 large marshmallows
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (12 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat marshmallows, milk, butter, and salt in a saucepan over low heat until marshmallows melt, stirring often, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add chocolate chips and vanilla extract; continue stirring until chocolate is melted, 5 minutes more. Serve warm.
